About

This Budget Tracker was inspired to help young people, especially foreign students like me, to be more responsible with money and educate themselves about budgeting. Moving to a new country as a student brings unique financial challenges - from managing limited funds to understanding different currencies and cost of living.

I realized that many of us struggle with financial literacy and often find ourselves overspending without proper tracking. This tool focuses on simplicity and education, helping users develop healthy financial habits through clear insights and easy-to-use budget management features.

Whether you're saving for tuition, managing daily expenses, or planning for the future, this Budget Tracker aims to empower young people with the tools they need to take control of their financial journey and build a secure foundation for their dreams.
